Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

It is assumed in this analysis that lack of healtcare positively correlates with health risk. A positive correlations is found between state's poverty level and the lack of healthcare.

A negative correlation is found between household median income and lack of healthcare. States with higher household median income have been found to show lower level of lacks in healthcare.

Correlation is not found between states' age median and the lack of healthcare; with state sampled tend to have 8% to 18% on lacks of healthcare at age median of 36 to 40.
